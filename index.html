FOUNDER & LEAD TEACHER

Hi! I’m Solange Hansen

I’m a lawyer from Argentina specialized in Immigration and Labor Law, and a Spanish teacher for foreigners. Since graduating as a Spanish teacher, I’ve had the privilege of guiding students from all over the world through private lessons and at the international Berlitz Institute. I’ve prepared them for a variety of exams, with a special focus on dynamic group classes.

I founded Latino Lighthouse with one clear mission: to help you move from “I understand Spanish” to speaking it with real confidence. As you know, the heart of any language is speaking it—both while learning and to maintain your fluency. With us, you’ll achieve this through full immersion from day one, through real conversations, role-plays, videos, and rich cultural context.

My goal is for you to enjoy the process to the fullest while making real, lasting progress. I want Latino Lighthouse to be a true community for you: a welcoming space where you can make friends, practice the language, and immerse yourself in the vibrant Latin culture.

Our Teachers

Highly qualified native Spanish teachers passionate about helping you speak with confidence.

Ricardo Tapia

Native from Colombia

Over 10 years teaching Spanish and training in bilingual education, tourism and culture. Ricardo combines pedagogical knowledge with a practical, human approach. He follows the CEFR and creates motivating classes focused especially on conversation.

Laura Reta

Native from Argentina

Over 20 years teaching Spanish to foreigners. Laura makes grammar fun and practical through games, real-life situations and Argentine culture. She also offers the unique “Spanish with Argentine Music” modality using tango, zamba and folklore.

Eugenia Petrel (Euge)

Native from Argentina

Lived in Germany for five years. This experience allows her to deeply understand the challenges German-speaking learners encounter. With more than 14 years teaching both Spanish and English, she creates classes that are close, encouraging, and enjoyable.

Florencia García

Native from Argentina
